// About
Advancing computational solutions for biomedical research

My research focuses on high-performance computing and bioinformatics solutions for oncology drug discovery, next-generation sequencing data analysis, and clinical applications. I develop scalable bioinformatics workflows to process large-scale NGS datasets efficiently, including applications in somatic variant calling and biomarker discovery.

I collaborate on projects applying machine learning models to genomic and clinical datasets, including EHR-based analyses for precision medicine. My work bridges computational and experimental sciences, fostering critical thinking and preparing students for research and industry roles.
